POSTER POWER

In alignment with the observance of Vigilance Awareness Week, the Art & Design club in association with IQAC,IIC and CSR proposes a creative awareness initiative titled “Poster Power – Anti-Corruption Poster Display.”

This competition aims to promote integrity and ethical values among students through visual expression and artistic creativity.Certificates of merit will be given to the top two posters.
